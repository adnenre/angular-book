title: Signals vs. Observables
sidebar:
  order: 3
  label: 12.3 Signals vs. Observables
---

| Feature     | Signals               | Observables           |
| ----------- | --------------------- | --------------------- |
| Simplicity  | Very simple           | More complex          |
| Reactivity  | Automatic             | Requires subscription |
| Performance | Optimized for Angular | Depends on RxJS       |
| Use case    | Local state           | Data streams          |

Signals are often used for local state, while RxJS is useful for asynchronous streams.
